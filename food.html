Danish Foods Smørrebrød

Smørrebrød is simply a slice of rye bread with various combinations of toppings such as pickled herring, roast beef, and eggs topped with mayo and shrimps.

Frikadeller

Frikadeller (Danish meatballs) are very popular in Denmark, and they are served both for lunch and dinner. Traditionally, the meatballs consist of equal calf and pork, flour, milk, eggs, onions, and spices.

Stjerneskud

Stjerneskud is the lesser-known but more extravagant smørrebrød, and one of the Danes' absolute favorites.
